Octopus facts

Let me tell you some facts about octopus.

Their tentacles have a mind of their own. Literally! Their complex nervous system allows the tentacles to operate intelligently indepdendent of their brain.

Octopus have incredible camouflages. Within the blink of an eye they can completely blend into their surroundings. Octopus can change the color of their skin at will and can even modify the texture of their skin for extra sneaky ability.
